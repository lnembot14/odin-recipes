Candied Yams

Description

A classic thanksgiving meal that will have the whole table wanting more. Here's our guide on how to make the best candied yams

Ingredients

  • 1 (29 ounce) can yams or sweet potatoes, drained
  • ¼ cup butter, cut into pieces
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 ½ cups miniature marshmallows

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place drained yams or sweet potatoes in a medium baking dish; distribute butter pieces evenly on top.
  3. Sprinkle with brown sugar and top with a layer with miniature marshmallows.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until sweet potatoes are tender and marshmallows have melted, about 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
